<h2 class="article" >Site Map</h2>
<p>The main experiences the app would need included:</p>
<ul>
<li>inputting data for a moment and outputting the visualization</li>
<li>board(s) for the collected moments for individuals</li>
<li>board(s) for collective moments of circles</li>
<li>summary highlighting the major themes of a period of time (week, months, years)</li>

    </ul>
